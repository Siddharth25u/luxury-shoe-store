Our Story

Founded in 2015, THE QUIET STEP began as a vision to create footwear that speaks through quality rather than logos

Humble Beginnings

What started in a small workshop in Florence has grown into a global symbol of understated elegance, without losing our artisanal roots.

The Philosophy

We believe true luxury doesn't need to announce itself. Our designs reveal their quality to those who appreciate fine craftsmanship.

Global Recognition

From Milan to Tokyo, our shoes have found their way into the wardrobes of discerning individuals who value substance over status symbols.

The Art of Craftsmanship

Each pair of THE QUIET STEP shoes represents over 200 hours of meticulous handcrafting by our master shoemakers in Italy. We use only the finest full-grain leathers that age beautifully with time.

Our signature Goodyear welt construction ensures durability while allowing for resoling, making our shoes companions for decades rather than seasons.

Our Materials

We source only the world's finest materials, treating them with respect to create footwear of uncompromising quality

Italian Leather

Our full-grain leather comes from small tanneries in Tuscany that have perfected their craft over centuries. Each hide is hand-selected for its character and durability.

Precious Metals

Our hardware is crafted from solid brass and sterling silver, developing a rich patina over time that tells the story of your journey.

Silk Linings

The interior of our shoes feature breathable silk linings that provide unparalleled comfort while wicking moisture away from the foot.
